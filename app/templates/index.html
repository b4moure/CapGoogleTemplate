Usually, to get a job as a minor, you have to go through many steps such as getting a work permit (which needs to be processed by your school), filling out a I-9 form or other legal mumbo jumbo. There are over 25,000 undocumented students in the Bay Area and some who just don't know enough about the legality of working as a minor. Many students need to provide for themselves or their family, either way this app is meant to support students in that process of getting a job by making it easier.
