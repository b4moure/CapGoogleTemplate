If you want to become an employer, you need to know...

Be nice.

You (most likely an adult) are hiring a teenager (most likely in school) please be respectful and kind to these young adults. They are still learning about life and thanks to you, they are gaining experience for their future. At the end of the day, you are in charge of makign sure your employee is safe and secure while working for you. Please provide a safe environment.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions that I hope can answer your needs.


1. How do I get started?

Click above where it says "sign up". This button will take you to a sign up page where you can start the process of signing up. It'll require some basic information such as your email, address, phone number, and more. This data is just used to help build your profile as an employer! Thank you for joining the team.


2. Where can I find out more information on a potential employee?

You can find more information through the employee of interests' profile. It will be similar to your profil however it will include the type of work they do, their work history and other significant details. This profile can be found here; Coming soon.
